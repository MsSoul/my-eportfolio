Trainings, Seminars & Recognitions

Research Adviser – Senior High School Research Program

School Year 2025–2026 | Holy Trinity College of General Santos City

I was awarded a Certificate of Appreciation by Holy Trinity College of General Santos City in recognition of my service as a Research Adviser for the Senior High School Research Program during the School Year 2025–2026.

In this role, I mentored senior high school students throughout their research journey by providing guidance on research planning, methodology, data analysis, documentation, and presentation. I assisted students in overcoming research challenges, ensuring their projects met academic standards while encouraging critical thinking, problem-solving, and effective communication.

This experience strengthened my leadership, mentoring, and analytical skills while allowing me to contribute to the academic growth and success of aspiring student researchers. It also reinforced my ability to communicate technical concepts clearly, collaborate with diverse individuals, and support projects from planning to completion.
