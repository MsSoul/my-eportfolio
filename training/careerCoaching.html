VIRTUAL CAREER COACHING

March 17, 2022 | HTC VIA ZOOM

On March 17, 2022, I participated in the Virtual Career Coaching session held via Zoom and hosted by Holy Trinity College of General Santos City. The session provided insightful guidance on career development, job readiness, and professional growth.

It was a valuable opportunity to learn about effective resume writing, interview strategies, and career planning from experienced speakers.

This event helped broaden my perspective on career paths and equipped me with essential knowledge to prepare for the professional world.
