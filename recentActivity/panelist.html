RESEARCH AND CAPSTONE PROPOSAL DEFENSE PANELIST

April 24, 2026 - College of Engineering and Technology Education, Holy Trinity College
Image Source: https://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=1535945961868401&set=pb.100063590364810.-2207520000&type=3

On April 24, 2026, I had the privilege of serving as a Research and Capstone Proposal Defense Panelist for the College of Engineering and Technology Education (CETE) at Holy Trinity College of General Santos City. As a member of the evaluation panel, I assessed research and capstone proposals from Information Technology and Computer Science students as they presented innovative software and hardware solutions for real-world applications.

My responsibilities included evaluating each group's research objectives, methodology, system architecture, technical feasibility, implementation strategy, and overall project relevance. I provided constructive feedback on software design, database architecture, system integration, and proposed technologies while identifying areas for improvement to strengthen the quality and practicality of each proposed solution.

Serving alongside fellow educators and industry professionals was both an honor and a valuable experience. Participating in the evaluation process allowed me to contribute to the academic development of future IT professionals while promoting high standards in research, software engineering, and innovation. It also reinforced my commitment to mentoring aspiring developers and supporting the advancement of technology-driven solutions within the academic community.
