LGU-GENSAN OJT CULMINATION (EXEMPLARY AWARD)

June 25, 2025 - Venue 88, General Santos City
Image Source: https://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=1535945961868401&set=pb.100063590364810.-2207520000&type=3

On June 25, 2025, during the On-the-Job Training (OJT) Culmination held at Venue 88, General Santos City, I was honored to receive the Exemplary Award from the Local Government Unit of General Santos City (LGU-Gensan). Among more than 300 student interns deployed across various departments, fewer than 20 interns were selected for this recognition based on their outstanding contributions and performance during the internship program.

The award recognized my development of the Property Plant and Equipment Tracking System (PPETS), formerly known as the Asset Distribution and Tracking System (ADTS), during my internship at the City Accountant's Office. Designed as a full-stack mobile asset management solution, the system was built using Flutter, Dart, Node.js, Express.js, and MySQL to streamline government asset monitoring, borrowing and returning transactions, transfer requests, notifications, and record management. By replacing several manual processes with a centralized digital workflow, the system improved the efficiency, organization, and traceability of asset management operations within the office.

Receiving this recognition was a significant milestone in my professional journey. It demonstrated my ability to apply software engineering principles to solve real-world operational challenges within a government environment while delivering a practical solution that created measurable value. More importantly, it reinforced my commitment to developing technology that improves organizational processes and serves the needs of end users through thoughtful and reliable system design.

It’s an experience I will carry forward—proof that with the right mindset and willingness to learn, even interns can make a meaningful difference.
