SOCI X CETE DONATION TO ALABEL NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL

Aug. 16, 2024 - Alabel National High School, Alabel, Sarangani Province

On August 16, 2024, I had the opportunity to participate in the SOCI x CETE Community Outreach Program at Alabel National High School in Sarangani Province. Organized through the collaboration of the College of Engineering and Technology Education (CETE) and SOCI, the initiative donated five new computer sets to the school to help enhance its technology resources and provide students with improved access to digital learning.

As one of the selected participants, I assisted in the preparation and turnover of the donated computer units while supporting the successful implementation of the outreach activity. Contributing to this initiative allowed me to take part in a meaningful effort to promote technology accessibility and support the educational needs of students within the local community.

This experience reinforced the importance of community engagement and demonstrated how collaboration between educational institutions and organizations can create a positive impact through technology. It also reminded me that, beyond developing software, technology professionals have a responsibility to help bridge the digital divide and empower future generations through access to quality learning resources.
