HTC 40TH FOUNDATION ANNIVERSARY COMMUNITY OUTREACH DAY

Nov. 16, 2024 - Purok Sarif Mucsin, Brgy. Baluan, General Santos City

On November 16, 2024, I participated in the HTC 40th Foundation Anniversary Community Day as a representative of the College of Engineering and Technology Education (CETE). The outreach program was held at Purok Shariff Mucsins, Barangay Baluan, General Santos City, through the collaborative efforts of Holy Trinity College, HTC Student Supreme Council (SSC), CBMA, CCJE, TESDA, and The Quill Publication to provide educational and community development initiatives for local residents.

As part of the organizing and volunteer team, I assisted in facilitating community seminars and activities, including a Basic Hygiene Seminar for children and a Disaster Risk Reduction and Management (DRRM) Seminar for members of the Fisher Folks Association. Supporting these initiatives allowed me to contribute to programs that promoted health awareness, disaster preparedness, and community resilience.

Participating in this outreach activity strengthened my appreciation for community service and the value of collaboration in creating a positive social impact. The experience reinforced my belief that professionals in technology and education can contribute not only through innovation but also by actively supporting initiatives that empower and uplift local communities.
