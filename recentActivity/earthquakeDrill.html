EARTHQUAKE DRILL AT LGU-GENSAN - CITY ACCOUNTANT’S OFFICE

March 13, 2025 - City Accountant’s Office, City Hall

On March 13, 2025, I participated in the Nationwide Simultaneous Earthquake Drill conducted at the City Accountant's Office, City Hall of General Santos City, as part of my internship. The activity was organized to strengthen workplace emergency preparedness and ensure that employees and interns were equipped with the knowledge and procedures necessary to respond effectively during earthquake emergencies.

During the drill, I practiced the standard "Drop, Cover, and Hold On" procedure and participated in the orderly evacuation to the designated assembly area. The exercise provided valuable hands-on experience in following established emergency response protocols while emphasizing the importance of remaining calm, alert, and coordinated during critical situations.

Participating in this safety initiative reinforced the value of disaster preparedness, teamwork, and responsible workplace practices. It also highlighted the importance of maintaining a culture of safety and readiness within both government institutions and professional work environments.
