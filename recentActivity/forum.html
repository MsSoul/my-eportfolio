3RD GENERAL SANTOS CITY NATIONAL STATISTICS MONTH RESEARCH FORUM

Oct. 3, 2025 - Notre Dame of Dadiangas University (NDDU), General Santos City
Image Source: https://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=1535945961868401&set=pb.100063590364810.-2207520000&type=3

Grateful and honored to be one of the Top 10 Research Paper Finalists out of 95 participants at the 3rd General Santos City National Statistics Month Research Forum, organized by the Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) – Sarangani Provincial Statistical Office in partnership with the Local Government Unit of General Santos City. The forum was held on October 3, 2025, at Notre Dame of Dadiangas University (NDDU), General Santos City. I am truly thankful for the opportunity to represent our school and present our computer science research study before researchers, professionals, government employees, and academic experts.

During the research presentation, I showcased our study, "Optimizing Screen Time Management for Children's Devices: Leveraging Token Bucket and Time-Based Algorithms in a Famie Parental Control System." I demonstrated the overall system architecture, explained the functionality of the Famie Parent and FamKid Child applications, and discussed how the Token Bucket Algorithm and Time-Based Algorithm work together to provide efficient and automated screen-time management.

Presenting our research to a panel of judges and fellow researchers allowed me to strengthen my technical communication skills while sharing the methodologies, system implementation, software architecture, and algorithmic design behind our full-stack solution. Being recognized as one of the student finalists among participants from various professional and academic backgrounds made the experience both rewarding and memorable.
