PSITS XII MOST OUTSTANDING ITE STUDENT FOR SY 2024-2025

13th PSITS Regional Convention
Image Source: https://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=1535945961868401&set=pb.100063590364810.-2207520000&type=3

Receiving the PSITS Region XII Most Outstanding ITE Student Award for School Year 2024–2025 during the 13th Philippine Society of Information Technology Students (PSITS) Regional Convention was one of the most meaningful milestones of my academic journey. I am truly honored and grateful to have been recognized among outstanding student representatives from colleges and universities across Region XII.

This prestigious recognition reflects the dedication, perseverance, and passion I devoted throughout my years as a Computer Science student. It represents not only my academic performance but also my active involvement in research, software development, leadership, and technology-related activities that shaped both my technical and personal growth.

More than a medal, this award serves as a reminder of the countless lessons, challenges, and opportunities that contributed to who I am today. I am sincerely grateful to my family, mentors, classmates, friends, and the faculty members who continuously believed in and supported me throughout my academic journey. This achievement inspires me to continue learning, growing, and making meaningful contributions to the field of Computer Science.
