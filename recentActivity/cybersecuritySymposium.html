SYMPOSIUM ON CYBER KEY SECURITY AND DATA PRIVACY

Feb. 26, 2025 - EMC 3 - JTA Building, Holy Trinity College (DICT R12 - SAR. PROV & GENSAN)

On February 26, 2025, I had the opportunity to serve as part of the organizing team for the Symposium on Cybersecurity and Data Privacy conducted by DICT Sarangani–General Santos at EMC 3, JTA Building, Holy Trinity College of General Santos City. The event brought together first- and second-year students from the College of Engineering and Technology Education (CETE) to learn about the importance of cybersecurity, data privacy, and responsible digital citizenship.

As a member of the organizing committee, I assisted in coordinating event activities and supporting the successful delivery of the program. The symposium featured discussions on the E-Gov Application, Republic Act No. 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012), and Republic Act No. 10175 (Cybercrime Prevention Act of 2012), providing participants with valuable knowledge about protecting personal information and navigating the digital world safely and responsibly.

Being part of this initiative allowed me to contribute to cybersecurity awareness efforts within the academic community while supporting the promotion of safe and responsible technology use. The experience reinforced the importance of digital literacy, data protection, and cybersecurity education in today's increasingly connected society.
