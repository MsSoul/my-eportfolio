CAREER AWARENESS DAY

March 12, 2024 - Veranza Activity Center, General Santos City

On March 12, 2024, I participated in the Career Awareness Day held at the Veranza Activity Center in General Santos City. The event was organized by the IT and Business Process Association of the Philippines (IBPAP) in partnership with the Department of Information and Communications Technology (DICT) and the Department of Trade and Industry (DTI), bringing together students, industry professionals, and organizations to discuss career opportunities within the Information Technology and Business Process Management (IT-BPM) sector.

The event provided valuable insights into current industry trends, in-demand technical skills, and the diverse career opportunities available in software development, information technology, and the digital services industry. Through discussions with professionals and participating organizations, I gained a deeper understanding of employer expectations, emerging technologies, and the competencies required to succeed in today's rapidly evolving technology landscape.

Participating in this career awareness program reinforced my commitment to pursuing a career in software development and encouraged me to continuously enhance my technical skills, expand my industry knowledge, and prepare for the transition from academic learning to the professional IT workforce.
