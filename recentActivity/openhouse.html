HTC OPEN HOUSE 2024

Nov. 21-29, 2024 - Holy Trinity College

During the HTC Open House 2024, held from November 21–29, 2024, I represented the College of Engineering and Technology Education (CETE) by exhibiting two of my software development projects: the CETE Quiz Game, a web-based educational application designed to reinforce web design concepts, and a Tic-Tac-Toe mobile application developed using Flutter.

Throughout the event, I demonstrated both applications to students, faculty members, and visitors while explaining their objectives, development process, user interface design, and underlying programming concepts. I discussed the technologies used in each project, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Flutter, and Dart, and showcased how these technologies were applied to create interactive and user-friendly applications.

Participating in the exhibition provided an opportunity to share my work with a wider audience while strengthening my technical presentation and communication skills. It also allowed me to demonstrate my passion for software development and highlight the practical application of programming concepts through projects designed for learning and engagement.
