RESEARCH PUBLICATION: AMERICAN JOURNAL OF SMART TECHNOLOGY AND SOLUTIONS (AJSTS)

Published in Volume 4, No. 2 (2025) - International Peer-Reviewed Journal
Image Source: https://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=1535945961868401&set=pb.100063590364810.-2207520000&type=3

I am honored to have our research paper, "Optimizing Screen Time Management for Children's Devices: Leveraging Token Bucket and Time-Based Algorithms in a Famie Parental Control System," successfully published in the American Journal of Smart Technology and Solutions (AJSTS), Volume 4, No. 2 (2025). The publication followed the completion of our Bachelor of Science in Computer Science degree in June 2025 and was accomplished in collaboration with my co-authors, Regine Karla J. Panilaga and Alic Jhos L. Magallamento.

The published study presents a full-stack parental control solution designed to improve children's digital well-being through intelligent screen-time management. As one of the primary developers of the project, I was responsible for designing and developing the Famie (Parent) and FamKid (Child) mobile applications, implementing the backend REST API, and developing the system's Token Bucket Algorithm and Time-Based Algorithm to automate device access control and daily screen-time management.

Publishing our work in an international double-blind peer-reviewed journal validates the technical quality and academic contribution of our research. This achievement reflects my expertise in full-stack software development, algorithm implementation, mobile application development, and applied computer science research while contributing practical solutions to modern digital parenting and device management.
