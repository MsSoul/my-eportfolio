CETE OJT CULMINATION (MOST OUTSTANDING OJT BSCS AWARD)

June 5, 2025 - Grand Summit Hotel, General Santos City
Image Source: https://www.facebook.com/share/p/1FBgon14qD/

On June 5, 2025, during the On-the-Job Training (OJT) Culmination of the College of Engineering and Technology Education (CETE) held at the Grand Summit Hotel in General Santos City, I was honored to receive the Most Outstanding On-the-Job Trainee Award for the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS) program. This distinction represents the highest recognition awarded to a Computer Science intern for outstanding performance, technical competence, professionalism, and commitment throughout the internship program.

The award recognized my dedication, work ethic, and contributions during my internship at the City Accountant's Office of the Local Government Unit of General Santos City (LGU-Gensan). Throughout the internship, I had the opportunity to apply my academic knowledge to real-world software development, contribute to meaningful digital transformation initiatives, and deliver solutions that supported the operational needs of the office.

Receiving the Most Outstanding OJT Award marked a significant milestone in my academic and professional journey. Beyond recognizing my technical abilities, it affirmed my commitment to continuous learning, professional growth, and developing software solutions that create meaningful impact. This achievement continues to inspire me to pursue excellence as I begin my career as a software developer.
