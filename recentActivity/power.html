LABAN GIRL POWER - KAPIHAN NI JUANA

March 21, 2024 - Veranza Mall, General Santos City

On March 21, 2024, I participated in the Women's Month celebration titled "Laban Girl Power: Kapehan ni Juana", held at Veranza Mall, General Santos City. The event brought together participants from various sectors to recognize the achievements, resilience, and valuable contributions of women while promoting awareness of gender equality and inclusive community development.

The program featured discussions, knowledge-sharing sessions, and community engagement activities that highlighted women's empowerment, leadership, and equal opportunities across different fields. Participating in the event provided valuable insights into the importance of fostering inclusive environments where everyone has the opportunity to contribute and succeed.

This experience broadened my perspective on the role of diversity, respect, and inclusion in both the workplace and the community. It reinforced the value of supporting initiatives that encourage equal opportunities, collaboration, and empowerment, principles that are equally important in the field of technology and professional development.
